About Us

Stubblify is a predictive analytics platform developed for the Punjab Safe Cities Authority (PSCA) to monitor and forecast stubble burning across regions. It enables data-driven interventions for environmental sustainability and helps assess the impact of related policies. Created by a team of developers and solution-finders, Stubblify harnesses AI and satellite data to support cleaner air and smarter governance.
